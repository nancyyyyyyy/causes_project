    "Awareness" is becoming a bad word in some autism/disability groups. Some are asserting that parents aren't supposed to talk or write about a disability that isn't their own, an argument that completely disregards the reality that some of our children may not ever be able to advocate for themselves. We are not mommy martyrs for telling about the hard days. We are not objectifying those with disabilities to make us feel better about ourselves when we share stories of accommodation and acceptance.

    Our goal is simple: to improve the lives of all Australians on the autism spectrum and the families who love them.
